How to use Mood Helper

Follow these 4 simple steps to get the most out of your wellness journey.

1

📸 Check your mood

On the home page, click Take Photo or upload a photo. Our AI will detect your emotion (Angry, Sad, Happy, or Calm) in seconds.

2

🎵 Get personalised support

You'll be guided to meditation sessions, curated music playlists, and workout videos tailored to how you're feeling right now.

3

🛠️ Use Quick Tools

Access your Daily Streak, Mood Dashboard, Mood History Calendar, and Emergency Support anytime from the home page.

4

📔 Track your feelings

After every mood check, save a note in your Mood Journal. Build streaks, see your trends, and grow your emotional awareness over time.
