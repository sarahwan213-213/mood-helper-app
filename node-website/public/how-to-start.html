How to Use Mood Helper

Step 1: Check your mood

On the home page, click 📸 Take Photo or upload a photo. The AI will detect your emotion (Angry, Sad, Happy, Calm).

Step 2: Get support

You will automatically go to a page with meditation music, relaxing songs, and workout videos tailored to your emotion.

Step 3: Use Quick Tools

Use Daily Streak, Mood Dashboard, Calendar, and Emergency button anytime.

Step 4: Track your feelings

After every mood check, you can save a note in your Mood Journal.
